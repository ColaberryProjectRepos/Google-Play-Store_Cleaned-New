Adding Year Slicer 15

  •  To Create the Year Slicer, from the visualizations pane select Slicer
  •    Under the Fields Pane from Google Play Store  select date table and choose Year
       Fill up figures provided below for the necessary fields
General: Properties, Size
  •    Height=107
  •    Width=204
  •    Position, 
  •    Horizontal=0
  •    Vertical=82
  •    Title=On
  •    Text= Year
  •    Font=Segoe (14)
  •    Text color=#118DFF
  •    Background= #FFFFFF
  •    Horizontal alignment=Center
  •    Effects, Background=off
  •    Background color=of
  •   Transparency=0%
  •  Visual boarder=on, color= #CCCCCC
Visual:
  •   Values, Font= Segoe (10), Font color=#252423,Bold
  •   Slider=On
