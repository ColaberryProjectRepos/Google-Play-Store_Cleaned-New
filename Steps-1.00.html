Problem Statement 1
According to Sensor Tower, the Google Play Store earned $24.8 billion in 2018, which represents a growth of 27.3 percent as compared to its 2017 revenue ($19.5 billion). In comparison, the Apple App Store earned almost twice as much money ($46.6 billion) in 2018, but that represents only 20.4 percent in growth year-over-year. Last year, according to Sensor Tower, both app stores were neck-and-neck for growth, with each platform growing by about 34 percent in 2017 as compared to 2016.
It should be noted that China — one of the largest markets for mobile apps in the world — isn’t included in the Google Play Store numbers above since Google apps are not available there. The Apple App Store, however, is available in China. In other words, if Google apps were available in China, the chart above would likely look very different.
