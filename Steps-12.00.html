Creating '7 Higher Installs Categories' column 12
Click on 'Google_data_cleaned Data' in the fields section and then right click and choose 'new column'. Enter formula from bellow and click the Check Sign to create that column.
7 Higher Installs Categories = 
IF(
    RANKX(ALL('Google_data_cleaned Data set'[category]), [Category Installs],,DESC) <= 7,
    'Google_data_cleaned Data set'[category],
    BLANK()
)
