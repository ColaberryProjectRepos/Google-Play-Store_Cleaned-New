Creating the KPI Logo 20
To insert the Google Play Store logo on the KPIs, follow the following steps
1. From the Insert tab , click on the image button and select Google Play Store image and click open.
2. In the Image select Style then in the Scaling Select Fill
3. In the format pane, go to general and enter the below properties.
  •    Height=30
  •    Width=36
  •    Horizontal = 0
  •     Vertical =185
  •    Title=off
  •    Effects->Background=off
  • Visual Border-off
  • Shadow-off
Make Four Copies and change the Vertical in Position For the First copy to
vertical= 294, Second Copy vertical= 403, Third copy vertical= 512,
Fourth copy vertical= 620.
