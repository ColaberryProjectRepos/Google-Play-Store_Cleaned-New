Adding Title and Logos 14
To insert the Colaberry logo, follow the following steps:

1.From the Insert tab , click on the image button and select the 
Colaberry logo and click open.
2. In the format pane, go to general and enter the below properties.
  •     Height=80
  •     Width=205
  •     Horizontal= 0
  •     Vertical =0
  •     Title=off
  •     Effects->Background=on
  •    Visual Border=off
To insert the Google Play Store logo, follow the following steps
1.From the Insert tab , click on the image button and select Google Play Store image and click open.
2. In the Image select Style then in the Scaling Select Fill
3. In the format pane, go to general and enter the below properties.
  •    Height=73
  •    Width=205
  •    Horizontal = 1075
  •     Vertical =0
  •    Title=off
  •    Effects->Background=off
  • Visual Border-off
  • Shadow-off

To add the Project Title, follow the following steps
1. From the Insert tab, click on the text box button, click inside the box and enter the title for the project as below
Title= Google Play Store
  •   Font= Segoe UI, center aligned,
  •   Color= #118DFF
  •   size=28
2. In the format pane, go to general and enter the below properties.
  •   Height=84
  •   Width=637
  •   Horizontal =372
  •    Vertical =0
  •    Title=off
  •    Effects->Background=on, Color= #FFFFFF   ,Transparency=25%
  • Visual Border-off
