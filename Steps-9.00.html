Adding a 'Date' column 9
-   Right Click the three dots by the table name
       -   Choose ‘New Column’
       -   Put the following DAX formula.
Date = DATE('Google_data_cleaned Data set'[update_year],'Google_data_cleaned Data set'[update_month],1)
