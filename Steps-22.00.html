Adding Visual Charts- Content Rating Chart 22

Steps
  • Click on an empty space on the canvas, and from the visualizations pane select Line and Stacked Column Chart
  •  Fill up figures provided below for the necessary fields.
  • Build Visual Tab:
  •  X-axis = Content_Rating
  •  Column y-axis = Installs
  •   Line y-axis =Total Apps YoY, Total Revenue YoY, Avg Rating YoY, Avg Rate.
  •   Tooltips = Total Install, Total Review, Total Revenue, Total Apps, Total Apps YoY, Total Revenue YoY, Avg Rating YoY.
  • Cross-Report: On
  • Keep all filter: On
Change the name of the fields in the Line y-axis as:
Total Apps YoY To Apps YoY%                               Total Revenue YoY To Rev YoY%  
Avg Rating YoY To Rating YoY%                             Avg Rate To Avg Rate
Some of install To Installs
