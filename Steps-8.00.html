Importing Data Into Power BI Desktop 8



Dataset Links:
Download dataset from Kaggle and save it under your desktop.
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/yash16jr/google-play-store-cleaned?select=Google_data_cleaned.csv
1.         Open Power BI Desktop
2.          Click on ‘Get data’ button.
3.         Select Text/CSV and click on connect.
4.          Select the ‘Google_data_cleaned.csv’ file and click on Open.
 A preview of the data will be displayed, click on ‘Transform’
